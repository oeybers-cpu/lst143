How We Protect Anonymity

How to Use

This tool generates a PDF of your peer evaluations directly in your browser:

  1. Select your group and your name
  2. Rate each teammate using the scale: 1 (Minimal), 3 (Adequate), 5 (Outstanding)
  3. Add optional comments for each rating
  4. Click "Download PDF Report" to save the evaluation
